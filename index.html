Schools

Kindergarten

The purpose of Montessori Is to recognize the crucial importance of a child's first six years of development. It is during this time that a child's powers of absorption are highest and lifelong attitudes and patterns of learning are firmly formed.

Primary

After graduating from Kindergarten, the children join primary after a good start from kindergarten. We offer the 8-4-4 cirriculum for the primary school.
It consists of lower primary (Grade 1 to 3) and Upper primary (Class 4 to 8).

About Vineyard

Vineyard school School was founded in 2000. it is a school founded on strong christian values, apart from provinding quality education that helps shape an individual in a dynamic world. In the formative/early years, it was basicaly a montessori kindergarten. After establishing the montessori Kindergarten, parents expressed the need to having their children continue with Primary education. The establishment of an 8-4-4 primay came about as a response to this need.
