Welcome to Primary

After graduating from Kindergarten, the children join primary after a good start from kindergarten. We offer the 8-4-4 cirriculum for the primary school.
It consists of lower primary (Grade 1 to 3) and Upper primary (Class 4 to 8).

At our primary we ensure quality education from our committed teaching staff who work round the clock to provide the best for our students.

In primary, the children get to participate in various extra curricular activities like ballet, roller-skating, dancing/choreography,drama, deabte, swimming, soccer and monitoring open-days and we also offer facilities like our fully equipped computer lab to facilitate e-learning.
